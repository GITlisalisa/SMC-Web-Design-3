Ervin, a former senior software engineer, became increasingly fed up with how many balls he had lost. Using his background in software engineering, Ervin created Blue Ironwood Technologies and developed the first BIT ball.

Blue Ironwood Technologies is committed to integrating the best of technology into golf tools that will optimize your results and help you play your best game. If anything, we'll ensure you will never lose another ball.
