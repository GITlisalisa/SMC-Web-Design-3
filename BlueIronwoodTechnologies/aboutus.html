ABOUT US

THREE MINUTES is not a lot of time to tell our story or find a lost golf ball, for that matter.

2:29

Per USGA rules, if you cannot find your ball within 3 minutes, you must take a penalty, and the ball is considered lost. A former senior software engineer, Ervin became fed up with how many balls he had lost because he could not locate them within the allotted time. Using his background in software engineering, Ervin created Blue Ironwood Technologies and developed the BIT ball.

Blue Ironwood Technologies is committed to integrating cutting-edge technology with golf products that will optimize your results and help you play your best game. If anything, we'll ensure you will never lose another ball.
